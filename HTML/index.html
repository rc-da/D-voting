Welcome to D-Voting


At D-Voting, we're dedicated to empowering individuals like you to participate in a secure,
transparent, and decentralized voting experience. Our platform harnesses the power of
blockchain technology to ensure the integrity and fairness of every vote casted. You can :

Connect your Wallet to Get Started

Step 1: Install MetaMask
If you don't have MetaMask installed, download it from the Official MetaMask website.

Step 2: Set Up MetaMask
Open MetaMask and follow the setup wizard to create your wallet.

Step 3: Connect Your Wallet
Return to the D-Voting website and click the link to connect your MetaMask Wallet.
MetaMask will prompt you to authorize the connection to D-Voting. Approve the connection.

Why Connect MetaMask Wallet?


Connecting your MetaMask wallet allows you
to securely cast your vote, ensuring the transparency
and integrity of the voting process.
